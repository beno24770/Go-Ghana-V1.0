## Content Sourcing Strategy

### Primary Sources (Most Reliable)

1. **Official Tourism Sites**
   - Ghana Tourism Authority
   - Regional tourism boards
   - Museum websites

2. **Academic Sources**
   - University of Ghana publications
   - Historical research papers
   - Anthropological studies

3. **Reputable Travel Guides**
   - Bradt Guide to Ghana
   - Lonely Planet Ghana
   - Culture Trip Ghana section

4. **Local Experts**
   - Ghanaian cultural organizations
   - Tour operators (Jolinaiko, Kaya Tours)
   - Local bloggers (e.g., "My Beautiful Ghana")

### Secondary Sources (Verify Before Using)

1. **Travel Blogs** - Cross-reference multiple sources
2. **YouTube Channels** - Ghana-focused travel vloggers
3. **Reddit** - r/Ghana for recent experiences
4. **TripAdvisor** - Reviews and tips (verify accuracy)

### Content Verification Checklist

Before adding any knowledge file:
- [ ] Information verified from 2+ independent sources
- [ ] Cultural sensitivity reviewed
- [ ] Dates and facts checked (especially historical)
- [ ] Prices updated within last 6 months
- [ ] Regional variations noted where applicable

## Writing Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Authentic**: Use local terms (trotro, chop bar, waakye)
- **Practical**: Focus on actionable advice
- **Respectful**: Honor Ghanaian culture and traditions
- **Honest**: Include both positives and challenges
- **Engaging**: Tell stories, use examples

### Structure Template

```markdown
---
id: unique_id
title: "Descriptive Title"
category: "Culture|History|Tips|Food"
tags: ["tag1", "tag2", "tag3"]
region: "Region or All Regions"
last_updated: "2024-11-30"
---

# Main Title

## Introduction (1-2 paragraphs)
Brief overview of the topic

## Main Content (3-5 sections)
### Section 1
Detailed information

### Section 2
More details

## Practical Tips
- Bullet points
- Actionable advice

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
What tourists often get wrong

## Pro Tips
Insider knowledge

## Related Topics
Links to other knowledge files (when implemented)
```

### Length Guidelines
- **Culture/History**: 800-1200 words
- **Tips**: 600-1000 words
- **Food**: 500-800 words
- **Quick Reference**: 300-500 words
